Privacy Policy

IronStep Safety LLC · Last updated: January 2026

IronStep Safety LLC is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Who We Are

IronStep Safety LLC is a locally owned safety footwear business located at 1804 Cargo Court, Louisville, KY 40299. You can reach us at support@ironstepsafety.com or +1 (502) 878-6529.

2. Information We Collect

  • Full name, email, and delivery address — for order processing and local delivery.
  • Phone number — for delivery confirmation and order updates.
  • Order reference — only if provided voluntarily.
  • Payment information — processed by our PCI-compliant payment provider. We do not store, access, or retain card numbers at any time.
  • Purchase history and product selections — for order records and customer service.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill your orders.
  • To coordinate and confirm local delivery by our internal delivery team.
  • To send order confirmation and delivery notifications by email or text.
  • To handle returns, exchanges, and customer service requests.
  • To comply with applicable legal and financial obligations.

4. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell your personal data to any third party.
  • We do not share your personal data with external shipping carriers or fulfillment services. All deliveries are handled entirely by our own internal local delivery team.
  • Payment data is processed exclusively by our PCI-compliant payment processor. We never see or store your full card details.
  • We may share information with legal authorities if required by law.

5. Data Retention

Customer records are retained for up to 3 years for legal and accounting purposes. After that period, personal data is securely deleted.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. To submit a request, contact us at support@ironstepsafety.com. We will respond within 10 business days.

7. Cookies

This website uses essential cookies only, required for basic functionality such as session management. We do not use advertising, tracking, or third-party analytics cookies.

8. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. All payment transactions are encrypted via SSL.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent update. Continued use of the website constitutes acceptance of any changes.

Questions? Contact us at support@ironstepsafety.com or +1 (502) 878-6529 · Mon–Fri 8 AM–5 PM ET.
