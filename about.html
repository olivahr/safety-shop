About IronStep Safety LLC

Locally owned. Louisville built. Safety focused.

Who We Are

IronStep Safety LLC is a locally owned and operated safety footwear business based in Louisville, Kentucky. We serve warehouse workers, logistics professionals, and industrial teams across the Louisville metro area.

Our mission is straightforward: provide reliable, high-quality safety footwear at honest prices, with fast local delivery and real customer service from people you can actually reach.

What We Do

We stock and sell our own line of OSHA-compliant safety footwear under the IronStep Safety brand, including steel toe, composite toe, and aluminum toe options for men and women. Every model in our catalog is selected for durability, comfort, and workplace safety performance.

Unlike large online retailers, we operate locally. Our delivery team handles every order directly, ensuring your footwear arrives within 3–7 business days without the uncertainty of national carrier networks.

Our Commitment

  • Honest pricing with no hidden fees
  • Safety footwear designed to meet ASTM F2413-18 standards
  • Local delivery by our own team within 3–7 business days
  • Real support from real people — not automated systems
  • 30-day returns on unworn items, no hassle
  • 90-day coverage on manufacturing defects
